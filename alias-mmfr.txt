.pdcconv05 CLD $time MMMX PDC $aircraft CLRD TO $arr OFF 05 VIA $ROUTE CLIMB AND MAINTAIN ALTITUDE $alt EXPECT FLIGHT LEVEL $cruise SQUAWK $squawk NEXT FREQ $freq MAINTAIN RUNWAY HEADING AND EXPECT VECTORS GATE 1 TO 51 CONTACT GND FREQUENCY $freq/ POSITION 52 TO 101 AND POSITION TB TA CONTACT $freq GND
